Update the Lawn Trooper website, plan builder, and promotional messaging to make the experience clearer, more premium, more flexible, and more conversion-focused.

IMPORTANT:
- Do NOT break layout, styling, responsiveness, forms, animations, routing, pricing logic, or existing functionality.
- Preserve the military-inspired premium brand voice.
- Preserve the strong yard imagery and polished optics.
- The site should feel like Lawn Trooper is helping customers choose the best plan for their yard, not forcing them into rigid packages.
- Keep the experience simple, high-value, trustworthy, and easy to understand for first-time visitors.

MAIN GOALS:
1. Keep the beautiful visual plan cards and imagery.
2. Make the plans easy to understand.
3. Remove confusion around upgrades and promotions.
4. Highlight strong bundled value without exposing internal pricing strategy.
5. Make the promotions feel real, valuable, and professional.
6. Keep the UI clean, motivating, and premium.

6. MULCH WORDING FIX
==================================================

Do NOT describe mulch using hard quantity limits like:
- 4 yards of mulch
- up to X yards

Instead, use more professional and flexible wording such as:
- Seasonal mulch refresh
- Mulch refresh for standard landscape beds

This prevents customers from feeling shorted if they need less, and avoids problems on very large beds.
